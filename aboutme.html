About Me

Hi there! My name is Nur Amierah Aizah, but you can call me Mira. Here are a few things about me:

I live in Simpang Renggam, Kluang, Johor.
I am 20 years old. Still young but learning and growing every day
I love writing, exploring ideas and finding beauty in the little things, like a street cat crossing my path or an elderly couple holding hands.
I believe in the beauty of love. I also enjoy solitude, movies like The Notebook, reflecting on life's mysteries and diving into deep conversations about life, fate and everything in between.
I dislike unfairness and the feeling of not knowing myself enough.
"To live is to suffer and there's no life otherwise". This is my way of embracing life, knowing that challenges make us stronger and more human.
